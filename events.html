Join us for the Team Remote Graphics Expert (Team RGE) 2024 Virtual Event, scheduled for the 15th of February 2024. It is an end-user computing community driven event packed with great content, more than ten outstanding speakers, and experts in GPU, remoting protocols, desktop as a service, virtual desktop infrastructure, AI, and more. Your time is well spent; you will learn what is hot and new in the end-user computing industry and, specifically, with AMD, Intel, and NVIDIA GPUs for VDI, DaaS, DEX and AI use cases. Also, we'd like you to learn what tools are in the IT Professional toolbox to design, set up, and maintain a modern digital virtual workspace. Truly an action and demo-packed event you don’t want to miss!

Two voices, one future - End User Computing, DaaS and GPU trends for 2024

Ruben Spruijt & Benny Tritsch

Dr. Benny Tritsch and Ruben Spruijt, Dizzion Field CTO will kick off this year’s TeamRGE event with overview of the latest developments in GPUs, Remoting Protocols, VDI, DaaS, collaboration, DEX and AI. You will hear about the latest trends, receive an overview of AMD, Intel, NVIDIA GPUs in public cloud and on-premises and the latest with regards to Amazon, Citrix, Dizzion, Microsoft, VMware and learn more about the technology that will be the future of End User Computing. It will be a can’t miss, zero-fluff, and information-packed session - welcome!

Media:

Unraveling the User Experience Puzzle

Ryan Ververs-Bijkerk & Eltjo van Gulik

In the ever-evolving landscape of Virtual Desktop Infrastructure (VDI), ensuring a seamless and great user experience is paramount. However, the intricacies and nuances of user experience within VDI environments can often feel like a complex puzzle waiting to be solved. This session aims to decode the puzzle and shed light on the various factors influencing the VDI user experience.

Media:

The best free tools to analyze User Experience with GPU-enabled VMs

Benny Tritsch

On his mission to produce repeatable benchmarking test results, Benny Tritsch uses a range of products and tools when investigating the influence of different VM types, GPU acceleration, noisy neighbors, varying network conditions and different remoting protocol settings. In his session, Benny walks you through his top ten list of free tools.

Media:

40 Minutes of Private AI: The story of COBIE

Johan van Amersfoort

In today’s world, where businesses have fully embraced the power of chatbots and Large Language Models (LLMs), many forget the risk of feeding public AI services with sensitive data. Organizations that do see the risk and choose to run LLMs in their own datacenter experience challenges because MLops teams have trouble to scale their infrastructure. This is where the newly announced VMware Private AI Foundation, that includes VMware vSphere with Tanzu and NVIDIA AI Enterprise, can help. In this session, Johan van Amersfoort (ITQ) will explain the basics of AI, the risks of using public AI services, the added value of VMware Private AI Foundation, show LIVE demos, and will introduce the world to Project COBIE. A must-see session for anyone who would like to kickstart their own Private AI Journey!

Deepdive with Azure NGadsV620 with AMD Radeon V620 GPU<

Joe DaSilva (AMD) & Ruben Spruijt

In this session, you will learn everything you need to know to use the brand-new AMD GPU-based instance in Azure. Its performance features, use-cases, performance data running 40+ applications, and user experience in LAN, WAN at FHD, and 4K resolution. Is NVIDIA leading the GPU game in Desktop as a Service with Hyperscalers, Azure, AWS and GCP? It's worth joining this session to learn more!

Pushing the Boundaries: Exploring the Science of User Experience and Latency Limits in Remote Desktops

Magnar Johnsen

Discover innovative methodologies for measuring response times in remote desktop sessions, focusing on the key factors that can be fine-tuned for optimal performance. Our case study will spotlight a real-world scenario: a company in Australia accessing remote desktops located in Norway. This session promises to be a blend of theory and practical insights, offering valuable strategies to enhance user experience in an increasingly connected world. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, a UX professional, or simply curious about the limits of human-computer interaction, this session will equip you with a deeper understanding and practical approaches to tackle latency challenges in global networks.

Media:

What's new in Remote Display Analyzer

Bram Wolfs & Ryan Ververs-Bijkerk

Remote Display Analyzer (RDA) is a widely used tool to troubleshoot and get insights in your Remote Display configuration. It's easy to use and allows you to understand your remote display configuration in a matter of seconds. In this session we will show you where we are with Remote Display Analyzer and what is coming.

Media:

How to get the best User Experience for 3D Workloads

Rody Kossen

Always wanted to know how to get the best user experience for (high-end) workloads? Look no further! In this session you will learn how to optimize Citrix HDX for various workloads and how adding a GPU can further optimize the user experience. We will talk about why it matters to look at GPU generations and encoder capabilities, which policies are the best for certain workloads and how to handle tough use cases like high packet loss. This session is applicable to both On-Prem as well as Cloud workloads!

Media:

Ruben's deep DaaS Analysis in Azure, AWS, and GCP: Understanding Performance and price - 2024 edition

Ruben Spruijt

Looking to enhance your virtual desktop experience? Look no further! Windows Desktops as a Service (DaaS) hosted on the public cloud infrastructure provided by the three major hyperscalers has become increasingly popular, offering a range of benefits including scalability and flexibility. Selecting the right VM types in Azure, AWS, and GCP, either with or without a GPU, can be challenging –your choice does have a huge impact on performance and perceived user experience! Join Ruben Spruijt an EUC geeks, as he explores the various options for utilizing DaaS and examine the application performance and best price/performance ratio across Azure, AWS and GCP. Discover unique and unpublished results using a modern DaaS solution and native browser apps running virtual desktops and applications in LAN and WAN environments. Gain valuable insights at this event on how to choose the right solution for your DaaS requirements. Don't miss this opportunity to optimize your virtual desktop experience!

Thomas Poppelgaard
