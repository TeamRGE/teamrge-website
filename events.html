Join us for the Team Remote Graphics Expert (Team RGE) 2024 Virtual Event, scheduled for the 15th of February 2024. It is an end-user computing community driven event packed with great content, more than ten outstanding speakers, and experts in GPU, remoting protocols, desktop as a service, virtual desktop infrastructure, AI, and more. Your time is well spent; you will learn what is hot and new in the end-user computing industry and, specifically, with AMD, Intel, and NVIDIA GPUs for VDI, DaaS, DEX and AI use cases. You will receive great insight into the latest remote protocol developments, delivering a great user experience for Amazon, Citrix, Dizzion, Microsoft, and VMware Virtual Application and Desktop solutions. Also, we'd like you to learn what tools are in the IT Professional toolbox to design, set up, and maintain a modern digital virtual workspace. Truly an action and demo-packed event you don’t want to miss!
